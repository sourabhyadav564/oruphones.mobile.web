Device Storage Check

Here is how you can check the storage capacity:

Step 1: Go to the Settings application

Step 2: Tap on General, and select iPhone Storage or About

Step 3: Scroll to find Capacity, and you shall see the storage details. There shall be a display of the space used by each application. 


Another method to check the storage on the iPhone is through connectivity with a Mac:


Step 1: On the Mac, open Finder/ iTunes based on the version

Step 2: Connect and Find the device

Step 3: A display of the total storage capacity can be seen. Hover the mouse and you shall see the capacity used by each content type.
