Device Storage Check

Here is how you can check the storage capacity:

Step 1: Go to the Settings application

Step 2: Tap on About Phone, or Storage if the option is available

Step 3: You shall see the storage details. If not, look for an All specs option and open it

You shall see the total space provided


Most devices shall have this information in different formats based on the device brand. Two alternate methods are:


Step 1: Go to the Settings application

Step 2: Tap on Device Maintenance

Step 3: Tap on Device care

Step 4: Find a Storage option below

Or,

Step 1: Go to the Settings application

Step 2: Tap on the search icon

Step 3: Type “Storage”


Find related search results and tap on them to view details
